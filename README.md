# Doctor Appointment Booking System

The appointment booking process typically involves the patient selecting a date and time that works for them, as well as specifying their reason for the visit. The system then checks the availability of the selected doctor and confirms the appointment if the doctor is available. The patient can receive a confirmation email or text message, and they can also be reminded of their appointment closer to the scheduled date and time.
